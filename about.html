The Journey

How I
Started

I started out in graphic design around early 2022. At the time, I was working in an electrical company and needed a design for a personal brand idea. I reached out to a designer, but the cost didn’t make sense to me at the time. That moment pushed me to try and learn design myself instead of outsourcing it.

“That’s how I got into graphic design—not from inspiration, but from necessity.”

As I learned, I began to understand layout, composition, and how visual decisions affect communication. But over time, I realized something important: Good-looking designs were not enough. Many products looked visually fine but still failed because users couldn’t understand them or didn’t enjoy using them.

That realization shifted my direction. I didn’t just want to design visuals—I wanted to design products that actually work. That’s what led me into product design around 2024, and by 2025 I fully committed to it.
